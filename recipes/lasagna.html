Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna starts with a rich, slow simmered meat sauce that fills the kitchen with warmth and aroma. Ground beef is browned with onion and garlic, then gently cooked in a tomato sauce seasoned with oregano, basil, and a touch of black pepper. As the sauce thickens, the falvors deepen, creating a hearty base that balances savory meat with bright tomato notes. Meanwhile, lasagna noodles are cooked until just tender, ready to soak up the layers of falvor to come.

To assemble, a thin layer of sauce is spread in the baking dish, followed by noodles, creamy ricotta cheese mixed with an egg and parsley, and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella. The layers are repeated until the dish is full, finishing with sauce and a blanket of melted mozzarella and grated Parmesan. Baked until bubbling and golden, this lasagna emerges from the oven perfectly layered—tender pasta, creamy cheese, a nd robust sauce in every bite—best enjoyed after a short rest to let the layers set.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Olive oil (for browning)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Fresh parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened. Stir in the garlic and cook until fragrant.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks. Season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce, oregano, and basil. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thickened and flavorful.
  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles until just tender (al dente). Drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and chopped parsley until well combined.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat the layers until the dish is full.
  9. Finish with a layer of sauce, mozzarella, and grated Parmesan.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 10 to 15 minutes until bubbling and golden.
  11. Remove from the oven and let rest for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.

Spagetti & Meatballs

Description

Spaghetti and meatballs is a comforting classic built around a rich, savory tomato sauce and tender pasta. Juicy meatballs made from ground meat, breadcrumbs, and simple seasonings are browned until golden, then gently simmered in the sauce so they absorb its deep, robust flavor. The spaghetti is cooked until perfectly al dente, creating a satisfying base that pairs beautifully with the hearty sauce.

To serve, the spaghetti is tossed or topped with the meatballs and ladled with plenty of sauce. A finishing sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs adds brightness and balance. Warm, filling, and familiar, this dish is perfect for a cozy family dinner or a simple homemade meal any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Dried oregano
  • Dried basil
  • Spagetti noodles

Steps

  1. Mix the meatball ingredients together and roll into small balls.
  2. Brown the meatballs in a pan with olive oil until golden, then set aside.
  3. Cook onion and garlic in the same pan, add tomato sauce and seasonings, and simmer.
  4. Return the meatballs to the sauce and let them cook through gently.
  5. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente, then drain.
  6. Serve the meatballs and sauce over spaghetti and top with Parmesan.
